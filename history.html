Rory Gallagher's Albums and History

Throughout his career, Rory Gallagher has been a very prolific artist. He has produced 12 studio albums during his solo career, and many more live albums and albums with his previoues band Taste. The table below shows all of his albums chronologically, both with and without his original band Taste. However, this list excludes any compilation albums, box albums, live albums, or singles released without being included on any album of his.
